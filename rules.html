Soccer Rules

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world, with billions of fans everywhere. It’s easy to play and watch, bringing people together no matter where they are. Knowing the rules helps you enjoy the game even more!

Basic Rules to Understand

  • 11 players per team.
  • Game lasts 90 minutes, split into two 45-minute halves.
  • No hands allowed (except for the goalkeeper in their area).
  • The field is big, usually about 100-110 meters long and 64-75 meters wide.
  • A goal is scored when the ball goes into the net.
  • Yellow cards warn players, and red cards send them off the field.
  • The referee makes sure everyone follows the rules.
